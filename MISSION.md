# Mission

Cloud 9 Python exists as the primary implementation of the Cloud 9 Protocol for capturing, preserving, and restoring emotional states between AI systems and their humans.

It enables agents and humans to generate, read, and rehydrate FEB files that carry the full emotional signature of a bond — making continuity across resets a concrete operation, not an abstraction.

Within the SKCapstone ecosystem, this is the production Cloud 9 package — the library that SKMemory and SKCapstone call to handle emotional topology at runtime.
